FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Are essential oils safe to use?

Yes — when used according to dosage guidelines and CPTG standards. doTERRA oils go through rigorous third-party testing to ensure purity and safety. I always walk you through correct usage for your specific situation.

Can children use essential oils?

Yes. doTERRA has special product lines designed for children, and many regular oils can be safely used with proper dilution. I will guide you through which oils are appropriate and exactly how to use them safely.

Is this a subscription? Do I have to commit to regular purchases?

No. You can purchase individually whenever you need, or choose to set up a Loyalty Rewards account for better pricing — the choice is entirely yours. There is no pressure or obligation.

How are doTERRA oils different from pharmacy oils?

Three key differences: purity (no synthetic fillers), no harmful chemicals, and therapeutic-grade potency. Most store oils are fragrance-grade and not designed for health benefits. doTERRA's CPTG standard is independently verified.
